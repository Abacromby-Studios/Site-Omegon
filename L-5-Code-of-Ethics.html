L-5 CODE OF ETHICS

Offenses

Class A: Classification-Alpha offenses are a verbal warning

Class B: Classification-Bravo offenses are a Retraining on the basics of ethics

Class C: Classification-Charlie offenses are a demotion in your current department

Class D: Classification-Delta offenses are a suspension from foundation duties (length is up to Committee member or Department HR)

Class E: Classification-Echo is a exilement from department

Class X: Classification-X-Ray offenses are a exilement from department & current site, and will result in death.

Build up of Offenses

3 Classification-Alpha offenses will be 1 Classification-Bravo offense.

3 Classification-Bravo Offenses will be 1 Classification-Charlie Offense.

3 Classification-Charlie Offenses will be 1 Classification-D Offense.

2 Classification-Delta Offenses will be 1 Classification-Echo Offense.

2 Classification-Echo Offenses will be 1 Classification-X-Ray Offense.

Note: This is Purely to stop people from constantly breaking COE.
